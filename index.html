About
Every day a Skylander will be chosen at random and it is your job to guess it! With each guess you will get traits that they share with the correct answer until you get it right! Note: this game does not include Eon's Elite variants, minis, alternative versions, lost island exclusives or magic items.
Details
This is a goofy game inspired by LoLdle, Minecraftle and of course Wordle. You can view the project on GitHub. The data for each character is gathered from the Fandom page by me, the script I used can also be found on the GitHub page.
Privacy
There are no trackers or ads on this website and all hyperlinks are written such that the website they direct to does not hear from me. I use a single (1) cookie to keep guesses after refresh (you can check this in your browser's web tools) and are not saved by me.
